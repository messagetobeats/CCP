Comer Crescent Tenants Association The Comer Crescent Tenants Association (CCTA) is a non-profit organisation created in 2015. It has been formed by a group of residents and lease holders (tenants) of the Comer Crescent Estate, in order to improve; the building, local area and facilitate better communication between tenants and the landlord (or landlord’s agent). The association represents its members with regard to; safety, security, services and general maintenance of the communal areas within the Comer Crescent Estate in order to reduce costs and maintain safety and security. CCTA also provides support to members, where practicable, liaising between Tenants and the Landlord (or landlord’s agent). Full membership is limited to ‘qualifying tenants’ as described within section 29. of the Landlord and Tenant Act 1985 and section 3. of the Landlord and Tenant Act 1987, however, associate membership is open to all residents. If you would like to know more please contact us using the ‘contact us’ page or email us direct at contact@comerta.org.uk.
